Education

I am a enthusiastic and optimistic person who always seeks to discover new things and to meet new people. I like to challenge myself and feel so proud when I achieve my goals.

In High School I was studying Latin and ancient Greek but the past was nothing for me but wonderful and interesting stories. What I wanted was to learn what was behind technologies and be part of this new era of Internet. I then decided to study Information Technologies and soon realized that there was a huge challenge among us: securing our data and systems.

After a year at Liège University, I decided to go to Haute Ecole Libre Mosane to study cybersecurity and I must say: that was the best choice I made. Once I graduated from my Bachelor, I needed to learn more about computer systems, so I decided to pursue my education with a Master degree in Computer Systems Architecture at Haute Ecole Namur-Liège-Luxembourg and Namur University. There I have improved my technical and soft skills in project management!
This was not enough for me, I had the opportunity to make one of my oldest dreams come true: living in another country. I went on an exchange in Sweden, at Halmstad University, where I had the chance to meet people from around the world and discover scandinavian culture. In fact, the picture above was taken in a fjord in Narvik (Norwegian Lapland) during a trip :).

In my final year, I went for a 10 weeks internship at KPMG Belgium where I made research on cyber threats on crypt wallets. I also wrote a master thesis on IoT security architecture.

And now, let's continue the adventure!
